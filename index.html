Vision

Princess Co was founded with a clear vision: to produce and supply top-quality marble products to global markets. Our commitment to excellence is evident in every aspect of our operations, from the selection of raw materials to the final delivery of our products. With unparalleled standards in color, consistency, water absorption, and strength, our marble products are sought after by customers worldwide.

Mission

At Princess for Export and Import, we are dedicated to leadership in our industry. By leveraging our resources and optimizing our processes, we strive to exceed our customers' expectations in terms of quality, cost-effectiveness, and timely delivery. Our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction drives every decision we make.

Future

While we currently supply products to prestigious customers across international markets, our ambitions extend far beyond. In the years to come, we aim to expand our presence into new territories, offering our superior products and services to even more discerning customers. With our relentless work ethic and cutting-edge technology, we are poised for continued growth and success.

Present

Princess for Export and Import is a renowned name in the import and export of marble and natural stones. As global suppliers, we pride ourselves on sourcing unique natural stones from a diverse range of countries. Our dedication to quality craftsmanship ensures that every marble and granite block or slab we supply meets the highest industry standards, guaranteeing customer satisfaction with every purchase.

Our Guarantee

We stand behind every product we deliver, striving to exceed customer expectations at every turn. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction is unwavering, and we will go above and beyond to ensure that our customers are delighted with their Princess experience.

Meet our CEO

Mohamed Gaballah, a visionary entrepreneur whose passion for business ignited at a young age. In 2005, he transformed his dream into reality by founding Princess Co., a leading company in the import and export of fine marble. As the CEO, Mohamed's dedication and leadership have driven the company to success, setting new standards in the industry.
